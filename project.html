Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is a professional football player from Argentina, born on June 24, 1987. He began his football career as a youth player for Newell's Old Boys in Argentina before moving to Barcelona's youth academy. Messi made his professional debut for Barcelona in 2004 and has since won numerous titles with the club, including ten La Liga titles and four UEFA Champions League titles. He is widely considered one of the greatest football players of all time and has won numerous individual awards, including seven Ballon d'Or titles.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional football player born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira. He began his football career with Sporting CP in Portugal and then moved to Manchester United in 2003. At United, he won three Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League in 2008. In 2009, he transferred to Real Madrid for a world-record fee and won four Champions League titles and two La Liga titles. In 2018, he transferred to Juventus and has since helped them win two Serie A titles. Ronaldo is also the all-time leading scorer for the Portuguese national team and has won numerous individual awards, including five Ballon d'Or titles.
